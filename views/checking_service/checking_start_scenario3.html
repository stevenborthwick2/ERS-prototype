Submit your end of year CSOP return

Use this service if you operate a Company Share Option Plan (CSOP) and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 35.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any options were granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled under this scheme during the tax year. If relevant, you’ll also need to provide group scheme information and report any alterations or variations to share capital that have been made in relation to the scheme.

If options have been granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS), CSV or Excel file.

The spreadsheet templates are available in these file formats.
