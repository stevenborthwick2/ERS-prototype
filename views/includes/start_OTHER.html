Submit your end of year Other return

Use this service if you operate an Other scheme and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 42.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any options were granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled under this scheme during the tax year. If relevant, you’ll also need to provide group scheme information.

If options have been granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.
