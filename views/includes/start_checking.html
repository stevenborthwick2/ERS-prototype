Employment Related Securities (ERS)

Check your spreadsheets

Use this service to check for formatting errors in the spreadsheet that you need to upload as part of your ERS annual return.

Checking your spreadsheets regularly allows you to identify and then correct formatting errors. This makes it easier to submit your return at the end of the tax year.

Before you start

You'll need to know which type of ERS scheme or arrangement the spreadsheet is for and know where the spreadsheet is stored.

Your spreadsheet needs to be an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.

The time it takes to check a file depends on the speed of your internet connection and the size of your spreadsheet.
