Submit your Company Share Option Plan annual return

Use this service if you operate a Company Share Option Plan and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 35.

Before you start

Depending on your circumstances, you may need:

  • to record any events related to the scheme in a spreadsheet (.ods format) or comma separated values (.csv) file.
  • the scheme organiser’s company name and address, Company Registration Number (optional) and Corporation Tax Reference (optional) (and those of any participating group scheme members)
  • to know any alterations made to key features of the scheme and know whether there’s been a variation in the share capital

If relevant, group scheme member details can be entered manually or uploaded in a .csv file (as appropriate).
