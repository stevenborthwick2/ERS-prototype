Submit your end of year SAYE return

Use this service if you operate a Save As You Earn (SAYE) scheme and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 34.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any options were granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled under this scheme during the tax year. If relevant, you’ll also need to provide group scheme information and report any alterations or variations to share capital that have been made in relation to the scheme.

If options have been granted, exercised, released (including exchanges), lapsed or cancelled, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.
