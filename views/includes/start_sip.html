Submit your end of year SIP return

Use this service if you operate a Share Incentive Plan (SIP) and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces form 39.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any shares have been awarded or have ceased to be part of the plan during the tax year. You’ll also need to add trustees and, if relevant, provide group plan information and report any alterations that have been made in relation to the plan.

If shares have been awarded or have ceased to be part of the plan, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.
