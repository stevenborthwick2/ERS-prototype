Example Company Plan CSOP 1 - Scheme reference: XY1100000608980

Submit your end of year CSOP return

Use this service if you operate a Company Share Option Plan (CSOP) and need to submit your return at the end of the tax year. This service replaces the need for you to use form 35 to file your return.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any options were granted, exercised, released or exchanged under this scheme during the tax year. If relevant, you’ll also need to provide group scheme information and report any alterations or variations to share capital that have been made in relation to the scheme.

If options have been granted, exercised, released or exchanged, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.
