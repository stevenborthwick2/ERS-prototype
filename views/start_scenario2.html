Submit your end of year CSOP return

Use this service if you operate a HM Revenue and Customs approved Company Share Option Plan (CSOP) and want to submit your return at the end of the tax year.

Before you start

You’ll need to know if any options were granted or exercised under this scheme during the tax year. If relevant, you’ll also need to provide group scheme information and report any alterations that have been made to the scheme.

If options have been granted or exercised, you’ll need to upload your spreadsheet as an OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS file).

ODS files are a type of spreadsheet that makes sharing or collaborating with government documents easier.
